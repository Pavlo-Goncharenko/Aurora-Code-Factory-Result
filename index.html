Our Services

We provide a variety of services depending on your needs

Kickstart

You're a founder with an outstanding idea that requires a creative touch and speedy prototyping? We're here for you! Our team is skilled in creating prototypes, rapidly learning from users, validating concepts, and adapting your product to better meet market demands.
  • Rapid prototyping and concept validation
  • Supporting you in finding the ideal product-market fit
  • Great for initial fundraising

Superb

You've got the funding secured, you now need someone to understand your product needs and deliver? That's where we come in, with a team that has extensive expertise in providing high-quality products on a worldwide scale.
    • Product management
    • Roadmapping
    • App (web+mobile)
    • Infrastructure
    • QA (automated)
  • Perfect for startups with secured funding ready to build high-quality products

Elevate

Do you have an established product and team you’d like to perform better? With our experience in setting up teams, in-depth analysis, and reducing technical debt we’re a perfect match.
  • Enhance the team you already have
  • Ideal for companies with established teams aiming to improve structure, speed, and reduce technical debt
